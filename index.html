Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Angkor Compliance

How does the AI-powered compliance monitoring work?

Our AI system continuously analyzes your factory data, documents, and compliance metrics to identify potential risks and violations. It uses machine learning algorithms trained on Cambodian regulatory requirements to provide accurate, real-time insights.

Is the platform available in Khmer language?

Yes! Angkor Compliance is fully bilingual with native Khmer and English interfaces. All features, reports, and notifications are available in both languages, making it accessible to all your team members.

How secure is my factory's data?

We take security seriously. Our platform is ISO 27001 certified and uses enterprise-grade encryption. All data is stored securely in Cambodia and we comply with local data protection regulations.

Can I integrate with my existing systems?

Absolutely! Angkor Compliance offers API access and custom integrations. We can connect with your existing ERP, HR, and production systems to provide a unified compliance view.

What kind of support do you provide?

We provide comprehensive support including email, phone, and live chat in both Khmer and English. Enterprise customers get dedicated account managers and 24/7 priority support.

How long does implementation take?

Most factories can be up and running within 1-2 weeks. Our team provides full implementation support, including data migration, team training, and custom configuration to match your specific needs.
